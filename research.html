NeurIPS 2025

Massively Parallel Imitation Learning of Mouse Forelimb Musculoskeletal Reaching Dynamics

Leonardis, E. J., Bhagat, S., Lobato-Rios, D., Pereira, T. D., Azim, E.

Presented at the NeurIPS 2025 workshop on Intrinsically Motivated Open-ended Learning. We utilize JAX and MuJoCo-MJX to achieve speeds of more than 1 million steps per second through the physics and RL environment, enabling massively parallel imitation learning of mouse forelimb musculoskeletal reaching dynamics.

arXiv 2025 · San Diego, CA
Preprint

MIMIC-MJX: Neuromechanical Emulation of Animal Behavior

Lobato-Rios, D., Leonardis, E. J., et al.

A neuromechanical modeling framework integrating stac-mjx and track-mjx for data-driven biomechanical control of animal bodies in physics simulation. MIMIC-MJX enables high-speed imitation learning from motion capture data through fully differentiable, GPU-accelerated simulation.

arXiv 2025
