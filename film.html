Alfred P. Sloan Foundation · NYU Tisch

VERSE — Science Advisor

Science advisor for VERSE, a short drama directed by Noam Argov and funded by a 2024 Sloan Foundation grant through the NYU Tisch School of the Arts Production Award. The film explores identity and trauma through a dual-reality narrative examining the psychological impacts of virtual experiences, the disconnect between online and offline selves, and how digital violence affects real emotional wellbeing. I consulted on the neuroscience and psychology of virtual embodiment, dissociation, and trauma processing.

Evolution of AI in Film and In Reality

I spoke at Comic-Con International on the "Evolution of AI in Film and In Reality" panel, sharing my work at the intersection of AI, neuroscience, and media. I discussed how modern AI influences storytelling, scientific communication, and ethics. The room was packed, and I'm grateful to Andrea Decker for organizing the event.
